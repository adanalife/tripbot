Log subscriber-lifecycle events (`subscribe` on `channel.subscribe`, `unsubscribe` on `channel.subscription.end`) and record `extra_miles_earned` on logout events — the community sub-grant + 5% subscriber-bonus portion of a session the login/logout pairing can't reconstruct. The events rollup now aggregates it into a new `user_rollups.extra_miles` column, so reconstructed display miles ≈ `events_miles + extra_miles` while `events_miles` stays the pure pairing base (drift alarm intact).

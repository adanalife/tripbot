**`figureOutCurrentVideo` finds the currently-playing video file natively in Go instead of shelling out to `bin/current-file.sh`.** Linux walks `/proc/<pid>/fd` and readlinks each entry; darwin shells out to `lsof -p <pid>` and parses the output in Go (the parser is split from the invocation so it's unit-testable). Behavior-neutral port, with new unit tests covering the cross-platform helpers, the linux fd walker, and the darwin lsof parser.
